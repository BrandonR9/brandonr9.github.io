Introducing "Game Dictionary" - Our Final Project!

  1. Memory Game:

    • A classic memory challenge where you match pairs of pictures to complete the game.

  2. Frogger:

    • Navigate back and forth across busy streets, avoiding cars to reach your destination safely.

  3. Tic-Tac-Toe:

    • The timeless game of Tic-Tac-Toe, simple and familiar for a quick and engaging match.

  4. Pokemon:

    • A personal favorite with a unique twist on rock-paper-scissors, featuring six different options. I poured my heart and soul into crafting its look.

  5. DodgeBall:

    • Use your mouse to skillfully dodge incoming balls, testing your reflexes and agility. A crowd favorite, and Michael's top pick!
